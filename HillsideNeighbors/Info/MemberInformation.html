Member Information Info

The Member Information Form adds or updates information in the Member Database. This database is intended to be shared among members of the and is intended to be used during emergencies, such as, earthquakes, evacuations, and wildfires. This database may be useful for neighborhood security purposes.

With this form you can store images of
  • yourself -- selfie,
  • your housemates -- people and pets you live with,
  • your living structure -- view of front entrance so we can locate you by your house image.
The following files available for download:
  • The current version of the Member Database in a comma-separate-variable (.csv) spreadsheet form
Additional files to download can be requested to be put on this list.
