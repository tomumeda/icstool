Block Damage Assessors Info

Block Damage Assessors work in pairs and make a quick but detail assessment of the damage in the Division. They inspect each residence of each block noting whether it needs attention by the ICS, e.g., injuries, damages, or dangerous situations. They also note 'We're OK' signs. They report their results to their Division Leader. Responsibilities:

  • Take direction from their Division Leader.
  • Has adequate protective gear and equipment for the task.
  • Works safely, but quickly (3 minutes/residence on average).
