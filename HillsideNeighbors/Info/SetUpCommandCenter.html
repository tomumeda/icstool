Setting Up the Command Center Info

The following describes the setup procedure and the operation of the Northside Emprep ICC.

  • Wait until at least 3 people arrive at the ICC to select an Incident Commander(IC).
  • First, the IC assigns the Logistics Section Chief who (with others) will organize the equipment in the cache as shown .
    • Incident Status Board,
    • Response Team Signs and Equipment,
    • Communications Table,
    • Computers, Notebooks, Walkie-Talkies
    • Generator
  • Second, the Logistics Section Captain assigns the Radio Operator who will ready the walkie-talkies, i.e., install batteries and set radio channel to use (17.17).
  • At this point all equipment and supplies should be set up and ready for use.
  • Third, the Radio Operator contacts the Division Captains and obtains the the number and names of all personnel at the Divisions who have not already Signed In and records this information into the ICS WEB Tool Volunteer Registration form.
  • Fourth, the IC ensures that the Operations Sections Chief and the Planning Section Chief are assigned.
  • Fifth, the Planning Section Chief assigns the Scribe who maintains the information on the Status Board
