Address Specification Form Info

There are two ways to specify addresses:

  • Previously reported addresses will appear in the 'Reported Address' list. You 'Select' one of these addresses.
  • You can specify the 'Street Name' and 'Address' of the location. You can choose from a list of known 'Street Names' or you can specify a 'New Street Name'. After specifying a street name (with 'Go'), you must specify an address for the 'Street Name' from the list of 'Known Addresses' or by specify a 'New Address'. A SubAddress field is provided if more precision is needed, e.g., apartment number, location on property.
