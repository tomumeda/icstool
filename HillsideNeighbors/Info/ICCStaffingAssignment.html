ICC Staffing Assignment Info

This form displays a table of personnel for the ICC Role displayed. Clicking on the Role button will take you to a description of the Role. The table lists the people signed in to the ICC (grouped by skill) with their current assignment. Select the names on the table to be assigned to the 'ICC Role'. Any person already assigned to that Role will be unassigned. The selected person will be unassigned their current Role and assigned to the one on this form. 'Submit' will make assignments and take you back to the Main Menu. Messages to everyone concerned will be sent.
