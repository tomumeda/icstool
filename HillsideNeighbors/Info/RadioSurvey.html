Radio Survey Info

This option allows you to enter your Radio Survey Observations and has two steps:

  • Specify the location of your observation by street name and address. You may select a previously reported address if you need to make changes.
  • Enter your radio survey data: Type of Radio, Signal Strength/Clarity, Location of Radio, and any Notes. If you are entering data for someone else and their name does not appear on the menu , please ChangeUser on the Main Menu to enter their name.
