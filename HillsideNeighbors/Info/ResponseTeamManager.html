Response Team Manager Info

The Response Team Manager assembles Response Teams which are deployed to neighborhood emergency situations.

  • Takes direction from and reports to the Operations Section Chief
  • Assembles Response Teams requested by the Operations Section Chief that are appropriate for the emergency situation
  • If there are injuries, include the Head-to-Toe exam skill on the team
  • Monitors the activities and status of the deployed Response Teams
  • Logs all communications and assignments.
  • Ensures that Response Teams are properly informed, equipped, and skilled for the situation and that they can operate safely.
  • Requests equipment needs to the Operations Section Chief.
