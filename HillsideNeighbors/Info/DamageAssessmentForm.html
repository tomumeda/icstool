Damage Assessment Form Info

The Damage Assessment Form is editable and displays

  • Address of the Damage
  • A button that takes you to a map showing the location of the address
  • The User Name giving the current report
  • A table of Damage Assessments
  • A space for notes
  • A button to display contact information associated with this address
  • A Submit button to record any changes
  • A Send Team button to take you to the Manage Response Team menu
  • A chronology of changes to the Damage Assessments
NOTE: If there are any SpecialNeeds for this address they will appear on the earliest Notes=.
