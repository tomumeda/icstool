Medical Triage Team Info

Medical Triage Team cares for the injured in the Medical Triage Areauntil City Emergency Services can take over.

  • Take directions from and reports to the Medical Group Leader on the conditions of patients.
  • Sets up Medical Triage Area.
  • Logs the medical condition of the injured on their Medical Triage Tag and in the ICS database.
  • Applies First Aid to patients who need it.
  • Monitors condition of patients.
