Shelter Manager Info

The Shelter Manager arranges temporary shelter for people who cannot stay in their homes.

  • Takes direction from and reports to the Equipment Manager.
  • Logs the people who are displaced (names, ages, location, contact information, etc.) and any changes to their situation into the ICS database.
  • Guides displaced people to appropriate shelters.
  • Monitors displaced people at shelters.
  • Provides supplies and personnel at the shelters.
  • Logs changes in availability of shelters, the space available, and the ICS personnel at the shelters.
